Frequently Asked Questions


FAQs

Have Questions? We Have Answers.

Who is eligible to take upskilling courses?

All Lehman College undergraduate students are eligible for 1 or 2-unit credit courses. Non-credit courses may also be available to non-Lehman students upon approval.

What technology do I need?

A computer and reliable broadband internet connection are required to participate in our online courses.

How much does it cost?

Upskilling covers up to 100% of tuition for eligible courses for Lehman College students enrolled full time. Contact us to learn more about your eligibility.

Contact us to learn more about your eligibility at blp.info@lehman.cuny.edu

How do I apply?

Lehman College students must register for their course on CUNYFIRST.

Other students must fill out and submit an application. FYI: an account must be created to apply first.
